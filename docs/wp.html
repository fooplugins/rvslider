Where's the plugin?

The Responsive Video Slider is built into the FooVideo extension for FooGallery. What is FooGallery you ask? It's a free WordPress gallery plugin that seamlessly integrates into the WordPress admin, check it out on WordPress.org, you won't be disappointed!
